About Me

Hello, my name is Eli and I am a design engineer from Poland.
I specialise in CAD design, EDA design, automation and industrial design, game design, but I also dabble in regular programming (HTML-CSS-JS, Python, Java, cpp).

I have a BEng degree in Mechatronics from Technical Univercity of Koszalin.

My intrests are in pair with my education, but also include photography (mostly analog B/W), tailoring and design.
